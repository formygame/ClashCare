About ClashCare

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind our organization

Our Story

ClashCare was founded by Nate Dickinson after seeing how many young people face conflict and mental-health challenges without support. With a background in youth development and community work, Nate created ClashCare to provide free mental-health services, conflict-resolution training, and safe spaces for young people.

Under his leadership, ClashCare has developed programs that combine counseling, workshops, and outreach events to equip youth with skills for resilience, empathy, and peaceful problem-solving. Nate's vision drives ClashCare to become a leader in youth empowerment and mental-health support.

Our Mission

"To empower young people to handle life's conflicts with confidence and compassion by providing free mental-health support, education in conflict resolution, and safe spaces for personal growth."

Our Vision

"A world where every young person feels supported, heard, and equipped to resolve conflicts peacefully, leading to stronger, more caring communities."
